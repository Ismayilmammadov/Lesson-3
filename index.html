About the Company

The company, started its activity based on the license issued by the Ministry of Economics of Azerbaijan Republic since 2012. Company offers fast and safe air transport of goods ordered through online stores, as well as printed matter, parcels, bulky and heavy outsize cargo to Azerbaijan from the USA, Germany, UAE, Turkey, China, England. Having a strong technical base and staff potential gives an opportunity to Company to work in the regions of Baku,Sumgayit and Ganja..
